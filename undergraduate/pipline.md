models for the evolution of polyploid wheats

	 - cultivated emmer -> *T. spelta*
	 - *T. durum* & *T. parvicoccum* -> *T. vulgare*
 4. transform:
	 - *T. spelta* -> *T. vulgare*
	 - *T. vulgare* with cultivated emmer -> *T. spelta*
